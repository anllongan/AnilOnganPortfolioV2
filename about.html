Who am I?

Born in 1998 in Izmir, I spent a significant part of my life in this beautiful city. After completing my primary and secondary education, my passion for aviation led me to Eskişehir Anadolu University. Graduating from the Aviation Management department, I gained various interesting experiences.

For 6 years, I worked as a paragliding instructor in Fethiye Ölüdeniz. During this time, my interest in sports aviation propelled me to the top, contributing to the development of my leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.

The last year has been a turning point in my life. Deciding on a career change, I embarked on a journey to develop and progress in the software world, which I initially pursued as a hobby. Currently, I am in the process of enhancing my skills in web development, and this journey is incredibly exciting for me.

The focus on a career in the software world is driven not only by the rapid evolution of technology but also by my passion for problem-solving and the desire to satisfy my creativity. My ultimate goal is to learn new things every day, improve myself in projects, and push the boundaries of technology.

In this portfolio, you can find more information about the services I offer, my projects, and my skills. I look forward to working with you and creating fantastic projects together. If you have any thoughts on collaboration or would like more information, please feel free to contact me.
